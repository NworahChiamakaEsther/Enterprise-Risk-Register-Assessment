# Third-Party Risk Management Policy  
StarLimited Inc

## 1. Purpose
This policy defines the approach for identifying, assessing, and managing risks associated with third-party vendors and service providers.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to:
- All vendors, suppliers, and service providers
- Cloud service providers and subcontractors

## 3. Third-Party Risk Objectives
- Protect sensitive data
- Ensure service availability
- Maintain regulatory compliance
- Reduce dependency-related risks

## 4. Vendor Risk Assessment
All third parties shall undergo risk assessment prior to onboarding, considering:
- Data access
- Service criticality
- Security posture
- Regulatory impact

## 5. Due Diligence
Due diligence may include:
- Security questionnaires
- Policy reviews
- Compliance certifications

## 6. Contractual Controls
Contracts shall include:
- Security and privacy requirements
- Incident notification obligations
- Audit rights

## 7. Ongoing Monitoring
Third-party risks shall be monitored throughout the vendor lifecycle.

## 8. Termination Management
Vendor access must be revoked upon contract termination.

## 9. Roles and Responsibilities
- Procurement: Vendor onboarding
- Risk Management: Risk assessment
- IT/Security: Technical evaluations

## 10. Policy Review
This policy shall be reviewed annually.
