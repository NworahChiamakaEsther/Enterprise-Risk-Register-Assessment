## Overview
This folder contains the core policies that support **enterprise risk management (ERM)** at StarLimited Inc.  

These policies provide governance, structure, and direction for how risks are identified, assessed, treated, and monitored across the organization. They are aligned with industry best practices such as **ISO 31000**, **ISO/IEC 27001**, and applicable data protection regulations.

The policies collectively address the organization‚Äôs key risk drivers, including:
- Increased cyber threat activity
- Dependence on third-party service providers
- Regulatory obligations related to data protection
- Operational reliance on system availability
