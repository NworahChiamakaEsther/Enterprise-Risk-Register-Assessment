# Risk Management Policy  
StarLimited Inc

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to establish a structured and consistent approach to identifying, assessing, treating, monitoring, and reporting risks across StarLimited Inc. This policy ensures that risk management supports business objectives, protects critical assets, and enables informed decision-making.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, and third parties acting on behalf of StarLimited Inc
- All business operations, systems, processes, and projects
- Strategic, operational, financial, compliance, and information security risks

## 3. Risk Management Objectives
StarLimited Inc aims to:
- Protect sensitive client and organizational data
- Ensure continuous availability of cloud-based services
- Meet regulatory and contractual obligations
- Reduce the likelihood and impact of adverse risk events

## 4. Risk Environment
The organization operates in an environment characterized by:
- Increased cyber threat activity
- Dependence on third-party service providers
- Regulatory obligations related to data protection
- High reliance on system availability and digital infrastructure

## 5. Risk Appetite
StarLimited Inc has a **moderate risk appetite**. The organization prioritizes:
- Protection of sensitive data
- Service availability and system resilience
- Regulatory compliance

Risks exceeding the defined risk appetite must be escalated and treated promptly.

## 6. Risk Management Process
StarLimited Inc follows a structured risk management lifecycle:

1. **Risk Identification** – Identify threats and vulnerabilities affecting business objectives.
2. **Risk Analysis** – Assess likelihood and impact using a defined risk matrix.
3. **Risk Evaluation** – Prioritize risks based on severity and appetite.
4. **Risk Treatment** – Apply appropriate treatment strategies.
5. **Risk Monitoring & Review** – Track risks and effectiveness of controls.

## 7. Risk Treatment Options
Risks shall be treated using one or more of the following approaches:
- Mitigate
- Transfer
- Avoid
- Accept

## 8. Roles and Responsibilities
- **Executive Management:** Oversight of enterprise risk posture
- **Risk Owner:** Accountable for managing assigned risks
- **IT & Security Teams:** Implement technical risk controls
- **All Employees:** Identify and report risks

## 9. Risk Register
A centralized enterprise risk register shall be maintained to document:
- Identified risks
- Risk scores
- Treatment actions
- Risk owners
- Review dates

## 10. Monitoring and Review
Risks shall be reviewed:
- Quarterly for high and medium risks
- Annually for low risks
- After significant incidents or system changes

## 11. Policy Review
This policy shall be reviewed annually or upon significant changes to the risk environment.
