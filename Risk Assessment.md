# Assessment Context

## Business Context
The organization provides cloud-based technology services to multiple clients and relies heavily on digital infrastructure and third-party vendors.

## Risk Environment
- Increased cyber threat activity
- Dependence on external service providers
- Regulatory obligations related to data protection
- Operational reliance on system availability

## Assumptions
- No formal enterprise-wide risk register currently exists
- Basic security and compliance controls are in place
- Risk management activities are performed inconsistently

## Risk Appetite
The organization has a moderate risk appetite and prioritizes:
- Protection of sensitive data
- Service availability
- Regulatory compliance
