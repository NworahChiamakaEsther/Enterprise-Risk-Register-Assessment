# Risk Assessment Folder

This folder contains all files documenting the **Enterprise Risk Assessment** process step-by-step. 

Each file represents a key stage of the risk management process:

- [Assessment Context](https://github.com/NworahChiamakaEsther/Enterprise-Risk-Register-Assessment/blob/main/RISK%20ASSESSMENT/assessment%20context.md): Business context, assumptions, and risk appetite.
- [Risk Identification](https://github.com/NworahChiamakaEsther/Enterprise-Risk-Register-Assessment/blob/main/RISK%20ASSESSMENT/Risk%20Identification.md): Detailed list of identified enterprise risks.
- [Risk Analysis](https://github.com/NworahChiamakaEsther/Enterprise-Risk-Register-Assessment/blob/main/RISK%20ASSESSMENT/Risk%20Analysis.md): Likelihood, impact, and risk scoring for each risk.
- [Risk Evaluation](risk-evaluation.md): Risk prioritization and evaluation results.
- [Risk Treatment Plan](https://github.com/NworahChiamakaEsther/Enterprise-Risk-Register-Assessment/blob/main/RISK%20ASSESSMENT/Risk%20Treatment%20Plan.md): Selected treatment options, owners, and actions.
- [Risk Monitoring & Review](https://github.com/NworahChiamakaEsther/Enterprise-Risk-Register-Assessment/blob/main/RISK%20ASSESSMENT/Risk%20Monitoring%20and%20Review.md): Methods to monitor risk and review the treatment plan.
