# Risk Treatment Plan

## 1. Purpose
This Risk Treatment Plan defines the actions selected to address identified risks following the risk assessment process.  
The objective is to reduce risk exposure to an acceptable level while aligning with organizational risk appetite and regulatory expectations.

This document supports compliance with:
- ISO 31000 – Risk Management
- COSO Enterprise Risk Management (ERM)

---

## 2. Scope
This plan applies to the following identified risks:
- Third-party vendor risk
- Ransomware and cyberattack risk

The treatment actions cover technical, administrative, and contractual controls.

---

## 3. Risk Treatment Approach
Risk treatment strategies are selected based on risk severity and organizational tolerance. The following treatment options are applied:

- **Mitigate:** Implement controls to reduce likelihood or impact

## 5. Detailed Risk Treatment Actions

### 5.1 RISK-TPR-001 – Third-Party Vendor Risk

**Risk Description:**  
Third-party vendors with access to organizational systems may mishandle or expose sensitive customer data.

**Selected Treatment Strategy:**  
Mitigation

**Treatment Actions:**
- Enforce SOC 2 Type II or ISO 27001 certification for high-risk vendors
- Conduct vendor risk assessments during onboarding and annually
- Implement least-privilege access controls for vendor accounts
- Include data protection and breach notification clauses in contracts
- Perform periodic access reviews and audits

**Residual Risk Expectation:**  
Likelihood reduced through access controls and monitoring; impact limited through contractual and compliance safeguards.

---

### 5.2 RISK-CYB-002 – Ransomware Risk

**Risk Description:**  
A ransomware attack could encrypt systems and disrupt business operations.

**Selected Treatment Strategy:**  
Mitigation

**Treatment Actions:**
- Deploy Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) tools
- Maintain offline and immutable backups
- Conduct phishing awareness and security training
- Implement incident response and disaster recovery testing
- Apply timely patch management for critical systems

**Residual Risk Expectation:**  
Likelihood reduced through preventive controls; impact minimized through rapid recovery capabilities.
