# Residual Risk Register

## Purpose
This register documents the **residual risk** for each identified risk **after treatment actions have been implemented**.  
It reflects the ongoing risk exposure, enabling continuous monitoring, reporting, and risk-based decision-making.

---

---

## Notes
- **Residual Score** = Residual Likelihood × Residual Impact  
- Residual risk indicates the **risk remaining after mitigation**  
- All risk owners must **monitor residual risk** and escalate if risk increases beyond tolerance  
- Updates to this register are reviewed **quarterly** or after major incidents
